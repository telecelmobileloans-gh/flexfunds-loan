▼

Does the service fee change based on amount?
▼
Yes, the service fee is a flat 5% of the loan amount. Use the calculator to see your exact fee before applying — no surprises.
Is this really interest-free?
▼
Yes. We use the Islamic Murabaha model — a fixed service fee only, zero riba (interest). This is fully Shariah-compliant financing.
How do I repay my loan?
▼
Repayment is processed through your Waafi or EVC Plus account. You will receive a reminder notification before the due date.
Is my personal data secure?
▼
Yes. We use bank-level encryption and strict security protocols. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
