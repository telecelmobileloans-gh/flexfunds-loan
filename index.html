Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my details be transmitted securely?

Yes. When you tap the final continue button, all your details — including the loan amount, duration, purpose, full name, phone number, monthly repayment estimate, and service fee — are securely passed to the verification step.

Is this offer available to all Telecel regular users?

Yes. This offer is intended for regular Telecel users in Ghana. The application form requires a valid Telecel phone number to proceed.

Does the calculator use Ghana Cedis?

Yes. The calculator operates entirely in GHS and supports Telecel loan estimates ranging from GHS 1,000 to GHS 50,000. All figures — including the service fee and total repayment — are displayed in Ghana Cedis.

How is the service fee calculated?

The service fee is calculated at a flat rate of 10.9% per month on the principal loan amount, multiplied by the number of months in your chosen repayment period. The calculator displays this fee transparently before you submit your application.
