Frequently asked questions

Who qualifies for a loan?+

Active Telecel Ghana subscribers with a registered Telecel Cash wallet and valid ID can apply. Your usage history helps determine your limit.

What is the loan range?+

You can borrow between GHS 500 and GHS 50,000, based on your transaction history and eligibility.

How fast is approval and disbursement?+

Approval typically takes about 15 minutes, with funds sent straight to your Telecel Cash wallet.

Are there any hidden fees?+

No hidden fees. All costs, including interest, are shown upfront before you confirm your application.

How do I repay my loan?+

Repayment is automatically deducted from your Telecel Cash wallet on the due date. Early repayment is also allowed at no extra charge.

Will my details continue securely?+

Yes. The final step opens a secure verification page carrying your amount, period, fee, and total repayment details.

Can I have two active loans?+

No. You can hold only one active loan at a time. After full repayment, you can apply again immediately.

⚠ Important notice: Rates shown are estimates and start from 10.9% based on your eligibility. Only active Telecel Ghana subscribers with a Telecel Cash wallet can apply. Approval is subject to assessment of your transaction history.
