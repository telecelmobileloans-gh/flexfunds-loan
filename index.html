Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I borrow?
+
You can borrow between GHS 500 and GHS 50,000, depending on your eligibility and chosen repayment period.
What are the repayment terms?
+
We offer flexible repayment periods of 1, 3, or 6 months. Pick whichever fits your budget — no penalty for early repayment.
How is interest calculated?
+
We charge 8.9% of your loan amount per month, for every month in your chosen term. A longer term means more months of interest, so the total cost is higher the longer you take to repay.
How long does approval take?
+
Most applications are approved within 2 minutes. Our instant verification system checks your eligibility in real-time, any time of day.
Is my information safe?
+
Yes. We use advanced encryption and strict security measures to protect your personal data at every step.
